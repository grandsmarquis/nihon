## Meaning

`гҖңгҒҢз—ӣгҒ„` means that a body part hurts. The body part comes before `гҒҢ`.

## Usage

Use a body part plus `гҒҢз—ӣгҒ„гҒ§гҒҷ`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Body part + гҒҢз—ӣгҒ„гҒ§гҒҷ | My body part hurts |

## Notes

Japanese often does not say "my" when the body part is clear from context. `й ӯгҒҢз—ӣгҒ„гҒ§гҒҷ` usually means "my head hurts" when the speaker is talking about themselves.
