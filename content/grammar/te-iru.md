## Meaning

`〜ている` describes an action in progress or a continuing state. In polite speech, it becomes `〜ています`.

## Usage

Make the verb `て` form, then add `いる` or polite `います`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbて + いる | is doing / continues to be |
| Verbて + います | is doing / continues to be, polite |

## Notes

For action verbs, `〜ている` often means "is doing now." For some verbs, such as `住む`, `知る`, and `結婚する`, it often describes a continuing state.
