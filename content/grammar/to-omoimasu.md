## Meaning

`〜と思います` means "I think that..." It lets the speaker give an opinion politely.

## Usage

Put the plain form before `と思います`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Plain form + と思います | I think that... |
| i-adjective + と思います | I think it is... |
| na-adjective / noun + だと思います | I think it is... |

## Notes

In real estate conversations, this helps learners state preferences without sounding too forceful.
