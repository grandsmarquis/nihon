## Meaning

`なら` means "if it is..." or "as for..." and responds to a topic someone has introduced.

## Usage

Put `なら` after a noun, phrase, or plain-form clause.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + なら | if it is noun |
| Plain form + なら | if that is the case |

## Notes

Use `なら` when choosing between options or giving information about a specific topic.
