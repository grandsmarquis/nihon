## Meaning

`〜ことができます` means "can do..." or "be able to do..."

## Usage

Use the dictionary form of a verb before `ことができます`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary + ことができます | can do |
| Verb dictionary + ことができません | cannot do |

## Notes

This pattern is slightly formal and clear, so it works well in workplaces and interviews.
