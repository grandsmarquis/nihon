## Meaning

`ので` gives a reason or cause. It often sounds softer and more explanatory than `から`, especially in polite speech.

## Usage

Attach `ので` after the plain form. With nouns and な-adjectives, use `なので`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Plain form + ので | because / since |
| Noun + なので | because it is noun |
| な-adjective + なので | because it is adjective |

## Notes

In very polite service situations, you may hear `ますので`, as in `写真を撮りますので`. This is common when staff explain the reason for an instruction.
