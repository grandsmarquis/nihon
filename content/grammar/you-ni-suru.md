## Meaning

`〜ようにする` means to try to do something, make an effort, or make something a habit.

## Usage

Use a dictionary-form verb or negative verb before `ようにする`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary + ようにする | try to do |
| Verbない + ようにする | try not to do |
| 〜ようにしてください | please try to do |

## Notes

This is common when giving health advice, study advice, or everyday habit suggestions.
