## Meaning

`〜さえ` emphasizes that something is enough, minimal, or surprising.

## Usage

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + さえ | 許可証さえ |
| Noun + でさえ | 経験者でさえ |
| Verb ます stem + さえすれば | 確認しさえすれば |

## Notes

`〜さえあれば` means if only someone has something. `〜でさえ` means even someone or something that the listener might not expect.
