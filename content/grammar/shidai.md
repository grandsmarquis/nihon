## Meaning

`〜次第` means "as soon as..." and sounds formal.

## Usage

Attach `次第` to the stem of a verb.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb stem + 次第 | as soon as someone does |

## Notes

This appears often in business emails and calls. It is more formal than `〜たらすぐ`.
