## Meaning

`〜前に` means "before..." It can come after a noun or a dictionary-form verb.

## Usage

Use `の前に` after a noun and `前に` after a dictionary-form verb.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + の前に | before noun |
| Verb dictionary + 前に | before doing |

## Notes

Use this to give an order of actions before meetings, trips, meals, or appointments.
