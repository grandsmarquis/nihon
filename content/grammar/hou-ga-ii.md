## Meaning

`〜たほうがいい` gives advice. It means "it would be better to do..."

## Usage

Use the past plain form of a verb before `ほうがいいです`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbた + ほうがいいです | It would be better to do |
| Verbない + ほうがいいです | It would be better not to do |

## Notes

Although the verb uses the past form, the advice is usually about what someone should do now or in the future.
