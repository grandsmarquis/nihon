## Meaning

`〜ばいいですか` asks "What should I do?" or "Would it be good if I...?"

## Usage

Use the conditional `ば` form before `いいですか`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbば + いいですか | should I do? |
| Question word + Verbばいいですか | where/what/how should I do? |

## Notes

This is very useful when asking for directions, procedures, or advice.
