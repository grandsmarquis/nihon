## Meaning

`〜てもいい` means "it is okay to do..." In question form, `〜てもいいですか` asks for permission.

## Usage

Make the verb `て` form, then add `もいいです`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbてもいいです | It is okay to do |
| Verbてもいいですか | May I do? |

## Notes

For a more polite permission request, you can use `〜てもよろしいですか`, but `〜てもいいですか` is common and useful in everyday situations.
