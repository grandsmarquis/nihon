## Meaning

`です` is a polite sentence ending used to state that something is true. It often works like "is" or "are" in English, but it is also a marker of politeness.

## Usage

Place `です` after nouns and な-adjectives in polite present-tense sentences.

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + です | 先生です |
| な-adjective + です | 静かです |

## Notes

Do not use `です` directly after plain verbs. For verbs, politeness is usually expressed with the `ます` form.
