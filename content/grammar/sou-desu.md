## Meaning

`〜そうです` reports something heard or read from another source.

## Usage

Use a plain form before `そうです`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb plain + そうです | I hear that... |
| い-adjective + そうです | I hear it is... |
| Noun / な-adjective + だそうです | I hear it is... |

## Notes

This grammar can also describe appearance with a different form, such as `降りそうです`. This page focuses on reporting information.
