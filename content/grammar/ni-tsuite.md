## Meaning

`〜について` means "about" or "regarding" a topic.

## Usage

Put `について` after a noun or noun phrase.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + について | about noun / regarding noun |
| Noun + についての + Noun | noun about noun |

## Notes

This is useful in interviews, recruiter calls, emails, and meetings because it clearly marks the topic you want to discuss.
