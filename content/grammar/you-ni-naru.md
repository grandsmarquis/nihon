## Meaning

`〜ようになる` means "come to..." or "become able to..."

## Usage

Use a dictionary form, potential form, or negative form before `ようになる`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary + ようになります | come to do |
| Verb potential + ようになります | become able to do |
| Verbない + ようになります | stop doing; become not to do |

## Notes

This is common when talking about progress, habits, and gradual change.
