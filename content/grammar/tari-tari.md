## Meaning

`〜たり〜たりする` lists example actions. It suggests "do things like..." rather than a complete list.

## Usage

Use the past plain form of each verb, replace final `た` with `たり`, then add `する`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbたり + Verbたり + する | do things like verb and verb |

## Notes

Even though the form uses the past shape, the whole sentence tense is decided by `する`, `します`, `した`, or `しました`.
