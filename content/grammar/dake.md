## Meaning

`だけ` means "only" or "just."

## Usage

Place `だけ` after the word or phrase being limited.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + だけ | only noun |
| Verb plain + だけ | only do; as much as |

## Notes

`だけ` is helpful for simple requests about food, quantity, and preferences.
