## Meaning

`から` gives a reason. It can mean "because..." or "...so..." depending on the sentence order.

## Usage

Place `から` after the reason.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Sentence + から | because sentence |
| Noun + だから | because it is noun |
| な-adjective + だから | because it is adjective |

## Notes

`から` is common in everyday speech. Compared with `ので`, it can sound a little more direct.
