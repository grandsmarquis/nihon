## Meaning

`〜ましょう` means "let's do..." It is used when suggesting an action that the speaker and listener can do together.

## Usage

Attach `ましょう` to the verb stem, the same stem used before `ます`.

| Verb | Stem | ましょう form |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 会います | 会い | 会いましょう |
| 食べます | 食べ | 食べましょう |
| します | し | しましょう |

## Notes

`〜ましょう` can sound decisive after both people agree. For a softer invitation before agreement, `〜ませんか` is often useful.
