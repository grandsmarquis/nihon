## Meaning

`〜あとで` means "after..." It connects two actions or events in time order.

## Usage

Use the past plain form before `あとで` for verbs. Use `のあとで` after nouns.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbた + あとで | after doing |
| Noun + のあとで | after noun/event |

## Notes

`〜あとで` focuses on what happens later. It is helpful for procedures with several steps.
