## Meaning

`〜ものの` means although or even though. It contrasts the first statement with a different or disappointing result.

## Usage

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Verb plain + ものの | 行ったものの |
| い-adjective + ものの | 高いものの |
| な-adjective + なものの | 便利なものの |
| Noun + であるものの | 初心者であるものの |

## Notes

This is more written or formal than casual `けど`, and it often appears when explaining problems, limitations, or unexpected outcomes.
