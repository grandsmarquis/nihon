## Meaning

`〜までに` means "by..." It marks the deadline for finishing an action.

## Usage

Put the deadline before `までに`, then say what must happen.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Time までに Verb | do Verb by Time |
| Date までに Noun を出す | submit Noun by Date |

## Notes

Do not confuse `までに` with `まで`. `までに` is for a deadline; `まで` is for continuing until a time.
