## Meaning

`〜ために` means "in order to..." or "for the purpose of..."

## Usage

Use a dictionary-form verb or noun + `の` before `ために`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary + ために | in order to do |
| Noun + のために | for noun |

## Notes

Use this when talking about goals, preparation, study, and work reasons.
