## Meaning

`〜つもり` expresses an intention or personal plan. It is useful when the speaker has already decided what they expect to do.

## Usage

Use the plain form of a verb before `つもりです`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary form + つもりです | I intend to do |
| Verbない form + つもりです | I intend not to do |

## Notes

`〜つもりです` often sounds more decided than simply saying `〜ます`. It focuses on the speaker's intention.
