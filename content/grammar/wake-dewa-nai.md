## Meaning

`〜わけではない` means "it does not mean that..." or "not necessarily..."

## Usage

Attach it after a plain-form clause.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb plain + わけではない | it does not mean that someone does |
| い-adjective + わけではない | it does not mean that it is adjective |
| な-adjective + な + わけではない | it does not mean that it is adjective |
| Noun + な + わけではない | it does not mean that it is noun |

## Notes

This is useful when you need to correct an assumption politely without sounding too direct.
