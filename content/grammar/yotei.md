## Meaning

`予定` means "plan" or "schedule." `〜予定です` says that something is planned or scheduled.

## Usage

Use the plain form of a verb before `予定です`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary form + 予定です | plan to do / be scheduled to do |
| Noun + の予定です | be scheduled as noun |

## Notes

`予定です` is useful for practical plans and schedules. Compared with `つもりです`, it can sound a little more schedule-based and less focused on personal intention.
