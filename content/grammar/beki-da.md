## Meaning

`〜べきだ` means "should do." It expresses a stronger judgment, duty, or expectation than casual advice.

## Usage

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary form + べきだ | should do |
| する + べきだ / すべきだ | should do |

## Notes

Use the polite form `べきです` in school and public-service conversations. It can sound strong, so pair it with `と思います` when softening an opinion.
