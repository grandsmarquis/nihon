## Meaning

`〜てもらえますか` asks "Could you do... for me?"

## Usage

Make the verb `て` form, then add `もらえますか`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbて + もらえますか | Could you do for me? |
| Verbて + いただけますか | Could you do for me? more polite |

## Notes

This pattern is practical in stores, hotels, offices, and other service situations.
