## Meaning

`〜ておく` means to do something in advance, prepare something, or keep a state ready.

## Usage

Attach `おく` to the verb `て` form.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb て + おく | do in advance |
| Verb て + おきます | polite form |

## Notes

In conversation, `〜ておく` often appears when arranging schedules, preparing documents, or preventing problems.
