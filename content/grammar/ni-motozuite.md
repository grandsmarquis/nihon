## Meaning

`〜に基づいて` means based on information, rules, evidence, data, or a plan.

## Usage

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + に基づいて | 記録に基づいて |
| Noun + に基づく + Noun | 規則に基づく判断 |

## Notes

This expression is useful when the speaker wants to sound objective and explain the basis for a decision.
