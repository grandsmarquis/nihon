## Meaning

`〜ことになっている` means that something has been decided, arranged, or set as a rule.

## Usage

Use the dictionary form or ない-form before `ことになっている`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary + ことになっている | it is arranged/rule that someone does |
| Verb ない + ことになっている | it is arranged/rule that someone does not |

## Notes

This expression keeps the focus on the arrangement or rule instead of who decided it.
