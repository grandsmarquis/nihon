## Meaning

`〜たことがある` means "have done..." or "have had the experience of..."

## Usage

Use the past plain form of a verb before `ことがある`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbた + ことがあります | have done |
| Verbた + ことがありません | have never done |

## Notes

Use this for life or work experience, not for something that happened only earlier today.
