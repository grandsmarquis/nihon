## Meaning

`〜てしまう` means that an action is finished or that something happened unfortunately or by mistake.

## Usage

Make the verb `て` form, then add `しまう` or the polite `しまいます`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbてしまいます | end up doing; do completely |
| Verbてしまいました | ended up doing; unfortunately did |

## Notes

In everyday speech, `〜てしまう` often expresses regret, such as losing, breaking, or forgetting something.
