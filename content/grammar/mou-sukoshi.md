## Meaning

`もう少し` means "a little more" or "a little bit" depending on the sentence.

## Usage

Put `もう少し` before an adjective, adverb, or request.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| もう少し + adjective | a little more adjective |
| もう少し + adverb + する | do a little more adverbly |

## Notes

This expression makes complaints and requests sound softer and more specific.
