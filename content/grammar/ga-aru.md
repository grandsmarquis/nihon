## Meaning

`〜がある` means that something exists. In health contexts, it can describe having a symptom or condition.

## Usage

Place the noun before `がある` or polite `があります`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + がある | there is / have |
| Noun + があります | there is / have, polite |

## Notes

For people and animals, use `いる`. For things, events, time, and many conditions, use `ある`.
