## Meaning

`〜ば` means "if" or "when" and sets a condition for what follows.

## Usage

Change the verb, い-adjective, or negative form into the `ば` conditional.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 書く -> 書けば | if someone writes |
| 分からない -> 分からなければ | if someone does not understand |
| 安い -> 安ければ | if it is cheap |

## Notes

Use `〜ば` for practical instructions, choices, and conditions at service counters.
