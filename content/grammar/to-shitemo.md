## Meaning

`〜としても` means "even if..." It presents a possible situation and explains what still applies.

## Usage

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Plain form + としても | even if |
| Noun + だとしても | even if it is noun |

## Notes

This pattern is useful for confirming exceptions, deadlines, and emergency rules.
