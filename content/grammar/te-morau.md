## Meaning

`〜てもらう` means that someone does an action for the speaker or for another person.

## Usage

Make the verb `て` form, then add `もらう` or polite `もらいます`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Personに Verbて + もらいます | have someone do for me |
| Verbて + もらいました | had someone do for me |

## Notes

Use this to describe receiving help or service, not receiving an object.
