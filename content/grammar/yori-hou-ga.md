## Meaning

`AよりBのほうが〜` means "B is more... than A." It is used to compare two options.

## Usage

Put the lower or reference choice before `より`, and the preferred or stronger choice before `のほうが`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| A より B のほうが Adjective です | B is more Adjective than A |
| A は B より Adjective です | A is more Adjective than B |

## Notes

This pattern is useful for comparing rent, room size, distance from the station, sunlight, and noise.
