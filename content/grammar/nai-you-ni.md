## Meaning

`〜ないように` means "so that ... does not happen" or "so as not to..."

## Usage

Use the negative plain form before `ように`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbない + ように | so as not to do; so that not |

## Notes

This is common in requests about allergies, safety, and avoiding mistakes.
