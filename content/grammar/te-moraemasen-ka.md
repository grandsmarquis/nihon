## Meaning

`〜てもらえませんか` means "Could you do... for me?" It is a polite request.

## Usage

Make the verb `て` form, then add `もらえませんか`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbて + もらえませんか | Could you do for me? |
| Verbて + いただけませんか | Could you do for me? more polite |

## Notes

The negative question form sounds softer and more polite than a direct request.
