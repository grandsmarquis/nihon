## Meaning

`ね` is a sentence-ending particle. It often shows that the speaker expects the listener to agree or understand.

## Usage

Place `ね` at the end of a sentence.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Sentence + ね | ..., right? / ..., isn't it? |
| Compliment + ね | Friendly compliment or observation |

## Notes

`ね` can make a sentence sound warmer, but it also asks for shared attention or agreement. It is common in small talk, compliments, and comments about the weather.
