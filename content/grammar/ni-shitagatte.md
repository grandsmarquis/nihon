## Meaning

`〜に従って` means according to something or following something.

## Usage

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + に従って | 規則に従って |
| Verb dictionary + に従って | 進むに従って |

## Notes

It can mean following a rule or instruction. It can also describe one change happening together with another change.
