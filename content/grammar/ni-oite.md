## Meaning

`〜において` means in, at, or during a particular place, field, event, or situation.

## Usage

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + において | 港において |
| Noun + においては | 船上においては |

## Notes

This is more formal than `で`. It is useful for rules, notices, official explanations, and public-service conversations.
