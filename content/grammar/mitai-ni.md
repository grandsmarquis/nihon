## Meaning

`〜みたいに` means "like..." or "in a way similar to..."

## Usage

Use a noun or plain phrase before `みたいに`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + みたいに | like noun |
| Plain phrase + みたいに | like the way something is |

## Notes

This is useful for describing a hairstyle, taste, design, or way of doing something.
