## Meaning

`〜たら` means "if" or "when" and is very common in spoken Japanese.

## Usage

Use the plain past form before `ら`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb past + ら | if or when someone does |
| い-adjective past + ら | if or when it is adjective |
| Noun/な-adjective + だったら | if it is noun/adjective |

## Notes

`〜たら` is flexible and natural for plans, instructions, and possible future situations.
