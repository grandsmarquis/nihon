## Meaning

`〜てください` means "please do..." It is used for polite requests, directions, and instructions.

## Usage

Make the verb `て` form, then add `ください`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbて + ください | Please do |

## Notes

`〜てください` is polite, but it can still sound like an instruction depending on the situation. It is very common at counters, offices, schools, and stations.
