## Meaning

`〜ことにする` means "decide to do..." The decision is made by the speaker or subject.

## Usage

Use a dictionary form or negative plain form before `ことにする`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb dictionary + ことにします | decide to do |
| Verbない + ことにします | decide not to do |
| Verb dictionary + ことにしました | decided to do |

## Notes

This is useful when changing plans because of weather, schedules, or new information.
