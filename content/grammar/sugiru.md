## Meaning

`〜すぎる` means "too..." or "do too much."

## Usage

Attach `すぎる` to a verb stem, an い-adjective without `い`, or a な-adjective stem.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb stem + すぎます | do too much |
| い-adjective stem + すぎます | too adjective |
| な-adjective + すぎます | too adjective |

## Notes

Use this when explaining a problem with size, price, heat, noise, or quantity.
