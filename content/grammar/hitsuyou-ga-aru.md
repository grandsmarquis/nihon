## Meaning

`〜が必要です` means "is needed" or "is required." It is common in service counters and application procedures.

## Usage

Place the needed item before `が必要です`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun が必要です | Noun is needed |
| Noun は必要ですか | Is Noun needed? |

## Notes

This pattern is practical for asking about documents, guarantors, fees, and application steps.
