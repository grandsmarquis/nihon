## Meaning

`〜ませんか` is used to invite someone to do something. It sounds polite and gives the listener room to accept or decline.

## Usage

Use the polite negative question form of a verb.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbます stem + ませんか | Would you like to do? |

## Notes

Although it contains the negative form `ません`, the meaning is not negative when used as an invitation. It is often softer than a direct suggestion.
