## Meaning

`〜に関して` means "regarding" or "concerning."

## Usage

Put `に関して` after a noun.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + に関して | regarding noun |
| Noun + に関する + Noun | noun related to noun |

## Notes

This sounds formal and is common in business emails, interviews, and HR conversations.
