## Meaning

`〜上で` means after doing something first, or based on a condition, rule, or confirmed information.

## Usage

| Pattern | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Verb た + 上で | 確認した上で |
| Noun + の上で | 話し合いの上で |

## Notes

This sounds more formal than `〜てから` and is common when explaining procedures, decisions, and safety steps.
