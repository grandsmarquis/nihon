## Meaning

`また今度` means "another time" or "some other time."

## Usage

Use it at the end or middle of a sentence when postponing or gently declining.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| また今度お願いします | another time, please |
| また今度しましょう | let's do it another time |

## Notes

This expression is polite and indirect, but it can be vague. Add a specific alternative date if you want to sound more committed.
