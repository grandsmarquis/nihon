## Meaning

`場合は` means "in the case that..." or "when..." and sounds practical or official.

## Usage

Place a sentence, verb phrase, adjective phrase, or noun phrase before `場合は`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb plain + 場合は | in the case that someone does |
| Verb past + 場合は | in the case that something happened |
| Noun + の場合は | in the case of noun |

## Notes

This is common in offices, banks, clinics, and other situations where staff explain rules.
