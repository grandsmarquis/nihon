## Meaning

`〜ないでください` means "please do not..."

## Usage

Use the plain negative form ending in `ない`, then add `でください`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbないでください | please do not do |

## Notes

This is useful for safety instructions, allergies, and polite restrictions.
