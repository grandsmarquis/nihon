## Meaning

`〜によって` shows that something changes depending on a factor, or that something happens by means of something.

## Usage

Put `によって` after a noun.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun + によって | depending on noun / by noun |
| Noun + によっては | depending on noun, in some cases |

## Notes

In job-search conversations, use it to explain differences by company, department, role, project, or situation.
