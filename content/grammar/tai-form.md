## Meaning

`〜たい` expresses that the speaker wants to do an action. It attaches to the verb stem, the same stem used before `ます`.

## Usage

Start with the polite `ます` form, remove `ます`, then add `たい`.

| Verb | Stem | たい form |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 行きます | 行き | 行きたい |
| 飲みます | 飲み | 飲みたい |
| 食べます | 食べ | 食べたい |

## Notes

`〜たい` behaves like an い-adjective after it is formed, so the negative form is `〜たくない`.
