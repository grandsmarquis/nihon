## Meaning

`〜かもしれない` means "might..." or "may..." It shows that the speaker is not sure.

## Usage

Use a plain form before `かもしれない`. In polite conversation, use `かもしれません`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb plain + かもしれません | might do; might happen |
| い-adjective + かもしれません | might be |
| Noun / な-adjective + かもしれません | might be |

## Notes

This is useful when explaining uncertain details, such as where something was lost or whether plans may change.
