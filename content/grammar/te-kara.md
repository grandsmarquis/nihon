## Meaning

`〜てから` means "after doing..." It clearly orders two actions.

## Usage

Make the first verb `て` form, then add `から`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbて + から | after doing |

## Notes

Use this when giving route directions, instructions, or step-by-step procedures.
