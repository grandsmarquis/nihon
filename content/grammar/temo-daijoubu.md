## Meaning

`〜ても大丈夫` means "it is okay even if..." or "it is safe to..."

## Usage

Make the verb `て` form, then add `も大丈夫です`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verbても大丈夫です | it is okay to do |
| Verbても大丈夫ですか | is it okay to do? |

## Notes

Compared with `〜てもいい`, this often checks whether there is a problem, risk, or concern.
