## Meaning

`いくらですか` asks "how much is it?" It is used for prices, fees, rent, and other costs.

## Usage

Place the item, service, or fee before `は`, then ask `いくらですか`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Noun は いくらですか | How much is Noun? |
| Noun は Amount です | Noun is Amount |

## Notes

In real estate, learners often need this pattern for rent, deposits, key money, renewal fees, and moving costs.
