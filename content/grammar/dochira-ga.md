## Meaning

`どちらが〜ですか` asks which one is more or better in some way when choosing between two options.

## Usage

Use `どちら` for "which of two." Put the quality after `が`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| どちらが Adjective ですか | Which is more Adjective? |
| Noun は どちらですか | Which is Noun? |

## Notes

`どちら` is polite and common with real estate agents. In casual speech, `どっち` is also used.
