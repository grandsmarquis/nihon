## Meaning

`〜とき` means "when..." or "at the time..."

## Usage

Use a plain form before `とき`. After nouns, use `のとき`.

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Verb plain + とき | when doing; when happened |
| い-adjective + とき | when adjective |
| な-adjective + なとき | when adjective |
| Noun + のとき | at the time of noun |

## Notes

Tense before `とき` helps show whether the action happens before or during the main action.
