## Meaning

`〜ということ` turns a sentence into "the fact that..." or "it means that..." It is useful when confirming an explanation.

## Usage

| Pattern | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Plain sentence + ということ | the fact that; meaning that |
| Plain sentence + ということですか | does that mean that...? |

## Notes

In conversations with school staff, this pattern helps confirm rules without sounding too direct.
