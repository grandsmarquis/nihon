## Teaching Notes

This conversation is harder because it asks learners to discuss values without sounding aggressive.

- `〜に関して` opens a broad environmental topic.
- `〜において` sounds formal when discussing public-space manners.
- `〜ものの` balances two values in tension.
- `〜としても` softens disagreement.
