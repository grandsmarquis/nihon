## Teaching Notes

This school-office conversation adds more formal confirmation language and exception handling.

- `〜について` introduces the paperwork topic politely.
- `〜ということ` confirms what a rule means.
- `〜によって` explains that requirements differ by situation.
- `〜としても` checks what still applies even if a deadline is missed.
