## Teaching Notes

Warranty follow-ups naturally use state and expectation language.

- `〜てある` describes repair-related preparation.
- `〜まま` says the problem is unchanged.
- `〜はずです` gives an expected completion.
- `〜てもらう` asks for a helpful action.
