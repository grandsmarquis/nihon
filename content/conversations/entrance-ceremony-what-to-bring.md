## Teaching Notes

This phone call practices formal logistics and careful confirmation before a school ceremony.

- `〜について` marks each topic: items, clothing, and reception.
- `〜ておく` is useful for advance preparation.
- `〜べき` asks what should be done.
- `〜によって` explains that customs differ by school.
