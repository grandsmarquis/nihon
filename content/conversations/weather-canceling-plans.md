## Teaching Notes

Friends use casual language while talking through weather risk and changing plans.

- `〜そうです` reports the forecast and other outside information.
- `〜かもしれません` keeps uncertain weather and transit details natural.
- `〜ことにする` marks the decision to cancel and make new plans.
