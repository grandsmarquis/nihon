## Teaching Notes

Second interviews often test fit, judgment, and communication style.

- `〜に関して` frames a formal interview topic.
- `〜わけではない` softens a potentially negative answer about overtime.
- `〜ようにする` describes work habits.
- `〜によって` explains that expectations change by team.
