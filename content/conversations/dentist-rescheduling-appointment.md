## Teaching Notes

Appointment changes need a reason, a soft tone, and a clear replacement time.

- `ので` explains the conflict politely.
- `かもしれません` leaves room for uncertainty.
- `〜ことにする` confirms the new appointment.
- `都合が悪い` softens the reason for rescheduling.
