## Teaching Notes

Follow-up visits often compare changes and give practical advice.

- `〜ようになる` describes a change in condition.
- `〜たほうがいい` gives advice.
- `〜かもしれない` explains possible causes.
- `〜ようにする` turns advice into a habit.
