## Teaching Notes

This conversation keeps the tone polite because the speakers are neighbors who know each other but are not overly casual. The grammar links focus on common beginner and lower-intermediate patterns that naturally appear in small talk:

- `〜ている` for an action happening now.
- `ね` for friendly observation and agreement.
- `〜つもりです` and `予定です` for talking about today's plans.
- Existing basics such as `は`, `です`, and `〜たい`.
