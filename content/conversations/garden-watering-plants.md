## Teaching Notes

This N5 conversation keeps the setting familiar and concrete: neighbors, flowers, vegetables, water, and soil. The grammar links focus on beginner patterns that appear naturally while caring for plants:

- `〜ている` for actions happening now and continuing states, such as watering flowers or dry soil.
- `から` for giving a simple reason.
- `〜てください` for polite garden instructions.
- `〜ましょう` for a friendly shared action.
- Basic sentence anchors such as `は`, `です`, `ね`, and `〜がある`.
