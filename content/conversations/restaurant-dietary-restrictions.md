## Teaching Notes

Restaurant allergy requests are a strong context for polite, clear limits.

- `〜ないように` asks staff to avoid an ingredient.
- `〜ても大丈夫` checks or gives reassurance about safe ingredients.
- `だけ` limits the order or restriction to one item.
