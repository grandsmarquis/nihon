## Teaching Notes

Neighbor complaints need soft wording and specific requests.

- `〜ようです` avoids sounding too direct.
- `ので` gives a reason gently.
- `もう少し` makes the requested change clear.
- `〜てもらえませんか` asks politely for cooperation.
