## Teaching Notes

Club activity questions combine permission, timing, and purpose.

- `〜とき` frames schoolwork and schedule situations.
- `〜ても大丈夫` checks permission and safety.
- `〜ために` explains the benefit of joining.
- `なら` responds to a specific option.
