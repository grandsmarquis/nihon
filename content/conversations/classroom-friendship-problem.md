## Teaching Notes

This conversation practices tactful language for a sensitive classroom issue.

- `〜について` introduces the concern calmly.
- `〜ようです` reports an observed situation without overclaiming.
- `〜わけではない` avoids blaming one side.
- `〜べきだと思います` gives a careful recommendation.
