## Teaching Notes

This conversation uses polite clinic Japanese between a parent, a child, and a doctor. It focuses on describing symptoms and understanding medical instructions.

- `〜が痛い` is useful for body pain.
- `〜があります` describes symptoms such as fever.
- `〜てください` appears in the doctor's instructions.
- `〜てもいいですか` asks whether an action is permitted.
- `〜たほうがいいです` gives medical advice.
