## Teaching Notes

This conversation uses careful, concrete language for a safety-related school topic.

- `〜について` introduces the allergy-accommodation topic.
- `〜ないように` explains prevention.
- `〜によって` handles menu-based differences.
- `〜としても` confirms what still applies when the child is absent.
