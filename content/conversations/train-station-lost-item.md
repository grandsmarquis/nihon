## Teaching Notes

This conversation practices explaining an accidental loss and giving uncertain details to station staff.

- `〜てしまう` shows regret about forgetting the umbrella.
- `〜たことがある` describes prior experience at the station.
- `〜かもしれません` lets the learner talk about uncertain places where the item may be.
