## Teaching Notes

Career-change interviews require careful explanation instead of simple enthusiasm.

- `〜わけではない` prevents the answer from sounding like a complaint about the previous job.
- `〜について` organizes interview topics.
- `〜ために` explains current preparation.
- `〜ようになる` describes the candidate's desired growth after joining.
