## Teaching Notes

Offer conversations require careful language because the learner must ask clearly without sounding demanding.

- `〜に関して` and `〜について` separate salary, start date, and documents as topics.
- `〜ことになっている` explains company rules around salary grades.
- `〜わけではない` softens negotiation language.
- `〜次第` gives a formal timeline for the final reply.
