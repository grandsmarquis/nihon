## Teaching Notes

This conversation uses shopping language at a higher difficulty by adding technical tradeoffs.

- `〜ものの` explains a limitation after a positive point.
- `〜に基づいて` asks for advice based on actual results.
- `〜によって` frames equipment choice as dependent on conditions.
- `〜上で` marks long-term maintenance as a selection criterion.
