## Teaching Notes

This public-service conversation raises the difficulty with eligibility rules and exceptions.

- `〜について` starts the formal inquiry.
- `場合は` frames administrative cases.
- `〜わけではない` softens a realistic concern about waitlists.
- `〜によって` explains decisions based on schedule and availability.
