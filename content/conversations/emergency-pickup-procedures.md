## Teaching Notes

This conversation combines emergency procedures with careful confirmation.

- `〜について` marks the safety topic.
- `〜によって` explains that procedures change by situation.
- `〜という` helps identify the exact message parents should look for.
- `〜べき` asks what action is expected in an emergency.
